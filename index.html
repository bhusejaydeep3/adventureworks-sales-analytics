Exponential Revenue Trajectory

Analyzing the 3-year historical dataset reveals a stark upward trend in overall company performance. By aggregating total sales data, we identified a massive 3x multiplier in gross revenue from the start of 2022 through the end of 2024, indicating strong market expansion and successful product scaling strategies.

Product Line Dominance

A deep dive into SKU-level performance highlights the overwhelming success of specific models. The Mountain-200 Black stands entirely apart from the rest of the catalog, generating over ₹44 Lakhs. This chart compares our star performer against other prominent models in the dataset.

Global Territory Distribution

Geographic analysis using SQL aggregations demonstrates clear regional preferences. The Southwest territory has secured the absolute #1 position in sales volume, capturing a significantly larger portion of the market share compared to other North American and International territories.

This analysis was powered by a robust SQL stack, transforming millions of rows of raw transactional data into structured, actionable business intelligence.
